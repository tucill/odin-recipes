Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a classic italian pasta dish often served with tomato sauce, meat, or vegetables.

The pasta has a tender texture, combined with a rich sauce that makes it a favorite among many. Spaghetti is easy to prepare and can be customized with various additional ingredients to suit your taste.

Ingredients

  • Dried spaghetti
  • Ground meat (optional)
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano or basil (optional)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the Spaghetti: Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente, according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Saute the Onions: Heat olive oil in a pan, then saute garlic and onions until fragrant.
  3. Add Meat(If Using): Add the ground meat, cooking until browned and fully cooked.
  4. Add Tomato Sauce: Pour in the tomato sauce, add salt, pepper, and oregano or basil if desired. Simmer the sauce until it thickens.
  5. Mix with spaghetti: Toss the cooked spaghetti into the sauce and mix until well combined.
  6. Serve: Serve the spaghetti hot, topped with grated parmesan cheese if desired.
