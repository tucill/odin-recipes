Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a popular dish made by stir-frying rice with various seasonings and ingredients like vegetables, eggs, and meat.

It has a savory taste with the richness of soy sauce, garlic, and other spices. Fried rice is a quick and delicious meal, often made using leftover rice to create a new, flavorful dish.

Ingredients

  • Cold cooked rice
  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • vegetables (cucumber, mustard, pickles)
  • Chicken or shrimp (optional)
  • Green onions (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Chop the vegetables and meat if using. Beat the eggs in a small bowl.
  2. Cook the Eggs: Heat some oil in a pan and scramble the beaten eggs until cooked. Remove and set aside.
  3. Saute Garlic: Add more oil to the pan and saute the garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add Vegetables and Meat: Add the vegetables and meat, stir-frying until they are partially cooked.
  5. Add Rice: Add the cold rice to the pan and mix well with the vegetables and garlic.
  6. Season with Soy Sauce: Pour in soy sauce and season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir until the rice is evenly coated.
  7. Add Eggs: Stir in the cooked eggs, mixing everything together.
  8. Serve: Serve the fried rice hot. garnished with green onions if desired.
